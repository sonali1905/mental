Mental wellness is a positive state of mental health. It is more than the absence of mental illness. Discover what
being mentally well means, and how it can help you lead a fulfilling life. No one experiences perfect mental
health or well-being all of the time. We all have to learn how to balance the different aspects of our lives.
When things in our life change, we have to re-establish the balance. However, ongoing mental health problems
can take a real toll on your well-being. Your mental health can affect many areas of your life like Work, school,
or home life Relationships with others Sleep Appetite Energy levels Ability to think clearly or make decisions
